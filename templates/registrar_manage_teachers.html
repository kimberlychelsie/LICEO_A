Add a teacher

Quick steps

  1. Fill in name, gender, and email. A username is auto-generated from the branch code and sent to the email on account creation.
  2. Pick a role — Adviser manages one advisory section/grade and also teaches subjects; Subject Teacher focuses on specific subjects across multiple grades.
  3. For Adviser: select the Advisory Grade, enter Subject Specializations, and set School Level. For Subject Teacher: enter Subject Specializations and set School Level only.
  4. Once the account is created, open Manage → Assign teacher in the list below to link them to specific subject slots and sections.
