Our Legacy & Mission

The History of Liceo

The San Pablo Diocesan Catholic Schools System (SPDCSS) traces its roots to a vision of providing transformative Christian education. For decades, Liceo has served as a sanctuary of learning, evolving from a local mission into a prestigious network of campuses across Laguna. Our history is a testament to the community's faith and our unwavering commitment to nurturing the next generation of God-centered leaders.

Legacy of Faith
Founded on the principles of service and spiritual formation for all students.
Academic Growth
Continuously evolving curriculum to meet global standards and future challenges.
Community First
Fostering strong relationships between parents, students, and educators.
Excellence
Maintaining the highest standards in Catholic education and character building.
